Internships

Internships are simulated work experiences typically offered to students or recent graduates who are looking to gain practical skills and knowledge in a particular field or industry. Internships provide an opportunity to apply academic learning in a real-world setting and make connections with professionals in the field. Here are some key points about our Internship Programs:

  • IndiTechSoft will provide a simulated work experience of a Software Development environment. The Internship will start a Software project with the Requirements Phase and finish with Software Delivery including Maintenance Phase.
  • Our internship program will typically cover the Project aspects like Requirements Definition, Estimation, Costing, Requirements Sign-off, Staffing, Project Management, Project Plan, review and control, High Level and Low-Level Designs, Development Models, Project Implementation, Software Testing, Software Delivery, Sign-off and Maintenance.
  • The highlight of this programme is that the Participants will get the exposure to full Software Development lifecycle phases.
  • The Internship Participants will be provided with relevant certificates on its successful completion.
  • These internships programmes are available on online mode and on offline mode (for colleges).
