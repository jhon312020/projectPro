Welcome to IndiTechSoft!!!

IndiTech Software Training and Solutions (IndiTechSoft), started by a group of experienced Industry veterans with the following objectives:

  • Job enabling of college degree students during their academic study period itself by exposing them to the niche technologies besides completing their final year (Main) and Second year (Mini Projects).
  • Exposing the students to the simulated Software Development environment through well thought out Internship programs.
  • Upskilling the Students, Working Software professionals and College Faculties in the niche software technologies through Workshops and Technology Trainings.

“Skilling made Simple” is the motto of our company.

IndiTech Software Training and Solutions (IndiTechSoft) has got the following offerings:

  • Projects:

    Projects are aimed to support the final year students to achieve the twin objectives of completing their academic main projects in the selected technologies and also upskilling themselves in the new technology during their academy timeframe itself.

  • Mini Projects:

    Mini projects are aimed to support the second-year students to achieve the twin objectives of completing their mini projects in the selected technologies and also upskilling themselves in the new technology.

  • Internships:

    These internships are aimed to support the degree students to achieve their internship requirements (simulated Software Development environment) and this is generally done in online mode.

  • Workshops:

    The workshops are aimed to expose both students and faculty members of the colleges with concepts, technology explanation and hands-on exercises in the niche technologies like Blockchain, Machine Learning and Cloud Computing (AWS). This is done at college premises.

  • Trainings:

    These Software trainings are aimed to provide end-to-end training to the Students/ Professionals in the niche technologies like Blockchain, Machine Learning and Cloud Computing (AWS) which includes hands-on projects and grading certificates.
