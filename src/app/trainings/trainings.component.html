Trainings

IndiTechSoft training has got twin objectives for college students:

  • Job Enable the Students during the academic period itself.
  • Complete the degree academic project as part of the training itself.

IndiTechSoft Trainings are guided by Training Made Simple concept. The Technical Topics will be explained with known simple analogies. Our trainings will have end-to-end approach having four levels viz: Concepts, Technology, Practicals and Projects.

  • These Software trainings are aimed to provide end-to-end training to the Students in the niche technologies like Blockchain, Machine Learning and Cloud Computing (AWS).
  • Every three Students will be designing, developing and executing a project in the selected technology.
  • Each participant will be provided with the relevant Certificate on completion of the training.
  • These Training programmes are available both on online mode and on offline mode (for colleges).
  • These training programmes are also available for working professionals. It is done through weekend Zoom sessions.
