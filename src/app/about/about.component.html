About Us

Indigeneous Tech Private Limited – IndiTech

  • Indigeneous Tech Private Limited started in May 2018 by a group of experienced Industry veterans with its Corporate Office at Bangalore, India. Its sole aim is to Job enable students pursuing degrees like BE (CSE/IT), BCA, MCA, BSc (CSE), etc… during their academic study period itself.
  • We provide Offline/ Online Projects based Trainings, Internships, Workshops in the Niche Technologies like Machine Learning, Blockchain, Cloud Computing, IoT, AR/VR etc. to College Students. Also, we provide Zoom based online trainings to Students and Professionals both during week days and weekends across the world.
  • Our college clients include BMS College of Engineering, Bangalore, National College, Jayanagar, Bangalore, Sairam Engineering College, Chennai, SRM University, Chennai.

Motto of our company is “Training is made Simple”.

  • We make sure that all the students learn, understand and excel in the selected curriculum/ Technology by way of our coaching methods with the core goal of “Technology is made Simple”.
  • Our Unique Value Proposal (UVP) is that to Job enable the students with our proven curriculum. That, is the students are trained (end-to-end) on the Technology selected with sufficient Hands-on practices, Executing Live Projects with required Project Documentation and achieving the Professional Certificate in the selected Technology.
  • For the benefit of remotely positioned and individually interested Students, we do provide Mini and Final year projects in the niche technologies with Hands On online support. Interested students can share your info with us and we will get back to you with details.

As these curriculums in Niche Technologies are executed during the academic period of the students itself, they are enabled for their Job before completion of their degree courses.

IndiTech has started this portal for the students so as to share their wealth of knowledge and experience that they gained during the last 20 years.
